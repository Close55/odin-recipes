Grilled Cheese

Description

Classic comfort food, delicious and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 2 slices cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat skillet over medium heat.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread
  3. Place 2 slices of bread, butter side down on skillet.
  4. Place 1 slice of cheese on each piece of bread
  5. Place top slices of bread, butter side up
  6. Cook each side until golden brown and cheese is melted
